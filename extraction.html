MODEL PERFORMANCE VISUALIZATION

In our heart attack risk prediction project, Model Performance Visualization is essential for evaluating the effectiveness of our machine learning algorithms. This section displays a graph that contrasts the actual ECG values with the predicted values generated by our model. The graph serves as a visual representation of our model's accuracy, enabling us to assess how well it predicts heart attack risks based on real-time ECG data. By comparing the predicted values against the actual measurements, we can easily identify areas where our model performs well and instances where it may fall short. This visualization is crucial for understanding the relationship between predicted outcomes and actual heart function. It allows for quick identification of discrepancies, which can highlight potential issues in the prediction process and guide further refinements of the model. Overall, the Model Performance Visualization not only enhances transparency in our predictive capabilities but also empowers users with insights into their cardiovascular health, fostering informed decision-making regarding their heart health risks.
