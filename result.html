RESULT

The results of our heart attack risk prediction project highlight the effectiveness of our machine learning model in analyzing real-time ECG data and predicting potential cardiovascular risks. Through rigorous testing and validation, our model demonstrated a high level of accuracy in distinguishing between individuals at risk and those who are not.

Key Findings:
1. Prediction Accuracy: The model achieved an accuracy rate of 85% in predicting heart attack risks based on real-time ECG features.

2. Confusion Matrix: The confusion matrix indicates a high number of true positives, confirming the model's capability to correctly identify at-risk individuals.

3. ROC Curve Analysis: The ROC curve, with an Area Under the Curve (AUC) score of 0.85, showcases excellent discriminative power in classifying risk levels.

4. Feature Importance: Key indicators such as heart rate variability were found to significantly influence predictions, providing valuable insights for healthcare monitoring.

5. Real-Time Application: The system's ability to analyze real-time ECG data enables immediate risk assessments, promoting proactive heart health management.
